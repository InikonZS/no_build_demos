About me

My name is Zhan and i am interested in programming as long time as i can remember. Graduation at Rolling Scopes School was a start point of my carrier of development. Finished this courses as student i continued to learn new technologies, and help for new students to get basic programming knowledge as a mentor. Also i got my first IT job instantly after completed course and got many skills of team development. My projects became bigger and bigger and my role at these projects grew up, i finished to count experence from first codeline.

So now i have 3 years of production development, have skills of team work, project architecture, fast code reading and debug skills.

My lovely language is TypeScript, and i like to write something hard with it, for example social media games, interactive applications, and much harder project means more interesting for me. Of course i write not only pure typescript, i always glad to learn something new interesting and solve any challenge task, especially this task is important for me or my job.

Waiting for new challenges for me.
